"Werifesteria"

Photography Project

Concept

The "Werifesteria" photography project captures the poetic journey of wandering through forests in search of mystery. Through evocative images of model @thaoan moving through sunlit woods, the series tells a visual story of discovery and hope. Natural light becomes both subject and metaphor, transforming ordinary landscapes into scenes of quiet wonder and emotional depth.

Impact

The project found remarkable success on Instagram, with all three posts reaching over 5,000 people and the companion video surpassing 1000 views. Its enigmatic captions and hauntingly beautiful aesthetic resonated deeply with audiences. The series demonstrated how compelling visual storytelling can create meaningful connections, inspiring viewers to explore their own journeys through nature.

"Ma Douce Muse"

Film Photography Project

About

This film photography series captures the fragile transition from adolescence to adulthood, a profound threshold of personal transformation. Shot on film with rich grain and layered light, the images employ dynamic angles and a warm, nostalgic palette to evoke both the energy and melancholy of coming-of-age. What began as a personal celebration evolved into a visual meditation on growth, where the tactile imperfections of film mirror the beautiful uncertainties of this life-changing passage. Through unconventional perspectives and textured compositions, the work invites viewers to contemplate their own journeys of becoming.

"Only You"

Music Video Project

Chỉ một câu - Thịnh Suy

Only You

This music video tells a heartfelt story of young love and missed connections. As the director, cinematographer, editor, and writer, I took complete creative control of this 4-minute project filmed over 4 hours from start to finish. The narrative follows a shy boy who believes his crush has accepted another suitor's gift of milk, when in reality she refused it. After this emotional misunderstanding, their honest conversation leads to a sweet confession and the beginning of their relationship.

Shot with a warm, nostalgic visual style reminiscent of film photography, the video captures the tender uncertainty of first love. The project earned perfect marks (10/10) and marked my first experience bringing a creative vision to life through directing, filming, and editing every element myself. Through its quiet moments and emotional payoff, the video celebrates both youthful romance and power of visual storytelling.
